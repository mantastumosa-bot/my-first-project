The benefits at a glance

What’s covered

  • Accidental damage
  • Breakdowns after the manufacturer’s guarantee ends
  • Unlimited repairs by expert engineers
  • A replacement if it can’t be fixed (with the same or similar features)
  • Two loss and theft claims for every 12 months of cover

What’s not covered

  • Loss of stored information, cosmetic damage, neglect, or deliberate damage
  • Costs from not being able to use your product or damage to other property
  • Cover for accessories (other than the standard accessories included with a mobile)
